F.A.Q.

How long does the interior design process take?

Our company tries to keep within the minimum time - 1 month. This month is needed to discuss your own ideas and wishes, select a team for repairs, as well as search for the right pieces of furniture and interior.

I can make repairs and design myself, then why do I need your company's services?

You need our company in order to facilitate the process of apartment renovation, as it is a time-consuming and complex process. Choosing us, you provide yourself with a beautiful and high-quality repair, a team of specialists will prepare everything for you, fully taking into account your wishes.

How much do your services cost?

You can get acquainted with the approximate prices of our company on our website using the example of ready-made designs. For more detailed advice and elaboration of an individual design, we suggest contacting the phone number or other contacts below.

How does the process of interior design development work?

To begin with, we meet with you to discuss the details, that is, what you want to see in your apartment, then we are already developing a 3D design so that you can get acquainted with how the design will look like, taking into account your wishes. After the approval of the project, we are already going with you to the facility with a team to start repairing the apartment of your dreams!

Is it possible to order from you just a design without repair of the apartment?

Yes, it is possible! You can contact our team with a detailed plan of your apartment and we will already develop the design together with you.
